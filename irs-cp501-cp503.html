IRS CP501 or CP503 Notice — What This Means

What This Means

A CP501 or CP503 is a reminder that the IRS has not received payment for a balance you owe. The CP501 is the first reminder, and the CP503 is the second reminder.

These notices indicate the IRS is escalating collection efforts. Ignoring them leads to more serious enforcement actions.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • The IRS will send a CP504 (final notice before levy)
  • Interest and penalties continue to accrue
  • The IRS may file a tax lien or initiate levy action
